ABOUT THE SAHARA GROUP

Company Profile

Our company was established in the year 2009. Our company is involved with various products supply and in imports and exports of commodities, borehole drilling, engineering services for food and beverages industries and educational sector.

Our Mission

  • We aspire to become a leading technology company delivering the best products and services to customers.
  • We exist to lead the industry in creating value for customers and SBH through differentiated services and technologies that save water and energy, enhance production and improve water quality, efficiency while reducing total costs of operation and education
  • We exist to fulfill the needs of our customers and understand through an enlightened partnership

Borehole Drilling

We currently have the capacity to do around 12 to 15 boreholes per month and we are expecting to expand the business more as and when we got more orders. We are highly experienced in borehole drilling and the directors are involved in this business since more than 20 years. We have two trucks of Ashok Leyland, India. One is mounted with compressor and one is mounted with drilling rig. We have a good team consisting of technical driller from India and other Kenyan staff consisting of 2 drivers, one welder, one geologist, one cook, one site supervisor and one office manager.

OTHER COMPANIES OF SAHARA GROUP

1. Shri Sai International LTD

Involved in various import and export activities like edible salt, rice and furnace oil. Our core business is collection of scrap metals like brass, copper, tense aluminum, radiators, stainless steel and grinding media balls and exporting them to India, China, Dubai, Indonesia and Djakarta. We also procure other scrap metals like batteries, soft aluminum, extrusions, cast iron scrap, high carbon steel scrap and heavy melting scrap and supply them to local industries.

Scrap metals are banned to be exported from Kenya and only a very few companies have scrap metals export licenses, ours being one of them.

Engineering Services: Turnkey Projects, Design and supply of Food and beverage industries, Stainless steel pipes and fittings.

2. Excel Global College

Which is involved in education business and teaches ACCA, CPA, Microsoft, ABE, Tally, Quick-books, amongst some of the courses. It is one of the only five colleges accredited by ACCA in Kenya. Our college has produced many Kenya and world toppers till now in ACCA, ABE and Microsoft Certifications.

Physical Address: High ridge, parklands, Nairobi.
